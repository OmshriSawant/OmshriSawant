## 💼 Work Experience

### 🏢 IFTAS — Security Engineer
**March 2025 – Present**

A big part of my role involves keeping security operations running smoothly across several key domains — from identity and access management to vulnerability scanning, SIEM, and firewall policy.

---

**🔐 Privileged Access Management · ARCON PAM**

Handled day-to-day privileged access operations — onboarding users and services, managing dormant accounts, unlocking access when needed, and setting up SFTP access for privileged users, making sure the right people had the right level of access at all times.

---

**🛡️ Compliance & Endpoint Security · Checkpoint EDR**

Monitored servers and workstations for compliance gaps, flagged non-compliant systems, and worked with relevant teams to get issues remediated before they could escalate — keeping the environment aligned with organizational security standards.

---

**🔥 Firewall Policy Management · FireMon**

Used FireMon to monitor and fine-tune firewall policies across the network. Collaborated with TAC teams whenever rule optimization or performance improvements were needed.

---

**🔍 Vulnerability Management · Tenable Nessus**

Helped roll out Tenable Nessus by installing agents on servers and keeping the vulnerability scanning infrastructure healthy through daily checks and maintenance — ensuring continuous coverage across the environment.

---

**📊 SIEM Administration · LogRhythm**

Onboarded servers into LogRhythm SIEM to bring more systems under log monitoring, expanding visibility and supporting the team's ability to detect and respond to potential threats.
